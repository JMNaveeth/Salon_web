Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book my appointment?

We recommend booking at least 24-48 hours in advance, though popular services and peak times may require more notice. For bridal services, we suggest booking 2-3 months ahead.

Do you offer consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary consultations for all our services. This allows us to understand your needs and recommend the best treatments for your hair, skin, and style goals.

What should I expect on my first visit?

On your first visit, you'll be greeted warmly and asked to complete a brief consultation form. Your stylist will discuss your goals and preferences, then provide personalized recommendations and pricing.

Do you have a cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may incur a fee. We understand emergencies happen, so please call us as soon as possible if you need to change your appointment.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! We offer gift cards for any amount. They're perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or just to treat someone special. Gift cards never expire and can be used for any of our services.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have free parking available on-site for our clients. Additional street parking is also available nearby. We're conveniently located with easy access from major roads.
